    Frequently asked questions

  • Limitations

    What are the types of limitations set by noTube?

  • What is noTube?

    noTube is a free online tool that allows you to convert YouTube, Dailymotion and other sites video to MP3 or MP4 format.

  • How does it work?

    Simply go to a YouTube video and copy/paste the link into the noTube homepage by selecting the preferred output choice.


    You can also replace the URL of a YouTube video by replacing the domain "youtube.com" with "notube.net" to start the automatic download in MP3 format.

  • Do I have to register?

    Nope. We want to make the use of our site as easy as possible. Therefore, no registration is required.

  • Can I use noTube on mobile/tablet?

    The noTube site is responsive. So you can use it on the media of your choice, PC, Mac, tablet or mobile phone.

  • Is the download anonymous?

    Of course, exchanges between you and noTube are encrypted using an SSL certificate. For your privacy, we do not keep any logs of the downloads made.

  • I have a bug or want to report an improvement!

    For suggestions or bug reports, you can contact us at click here.

  • How many files can I download?

    As much as you want. There is no limitation on the number of files to download.

  • How long does the download link last?

    Download links expire after 20 minutes. Feel free to download again if your link has expired.

  • What are the audio/video formats available?

    Currently noTube supports MP3, MP4, MP4 HD, 3GP, FLV and M4A formats. However, do not hesitate to contact us at contact us at for any suggestions for additions!

  • What can be the maximum size of a video to download?

    In order to maintain the best possible throughput, we allow files to be downloaded from 4 GB as much as possible.

  • Is it possible to download a live streaming?

    It's not possible to download a video from a live streaming at the moment.

  • I get the message "This video is copyrighted."!

    This message is quite normal. You are trying to download content that is protected by copyright and noTube does not allow such a thing. We strive to block the downloading of protected content in order to ensure the sustainability of the site.
